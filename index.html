About Us

We are a small family-run local business, located and operating in Cairville for 5 years. With over 25 years of experience in floristry, we offer exceptional quality and choice of fresh flowers & foliage tailor-made to suit any occasion. Take advantage of our next-day home delivery service (order by 3pm the day before).
